## E2 — Venue Map & Discovery
**Goal:** Allow users to browse an interactive map of pubs, breweries, wineries, and terraces across Romania. Each venue has type, rating, specialty, review count, and address. Anyone can add a new venue; community flagging handles moderation.
**Status:** Not started
**Tasks:** To be sliced by @meto-pm

---

## E3 — Reviews & Ratings
**Goal:** Enable authenticated users to submit, edit, and view star ratings and text reviews for venues. Reviews are tied to user profiles and displayed on venue pages.
**Status:** Not started
**Tasks:** To be sliced by @meto-pm

---

## E4 — Drink Tracker
**Goal:** Give each user a personal drink log (beer, wine, spirits) with producer, location, rating, and notes. Visualize progress as a filling glass/mug. Split by category. This is the Untappd-equivalent for the Romanian market.
**Status:** Not started
**Tasks:** To be sliced by @meto-pm

---

## E5 — Betiv Profile & ANBR Card
**Goal:** Give each user a public "Profilul de betiv" — an ANBR membership card with unique number, grade (Stagiar / Autorizat / Emerit / Academician), statistics, and earned badges. The card is downloadable as a PNG for WhatsApp/Instagram sharing. This is the platform's primary viral growth loop.
**Status:** Not started
**Tasks:** To be sliced by @meto-pm

---

## E6 — Romanian Producers Directory
**Goal:** Catalog Romanian breweries and wineries with their products. Each producer has a page linked from the drink tracker — when a user logs a beer, they can tap through to the producer's profile.
**Status:** Not started
**Tasks:** To be sliced by @meto-pm
